GOAL

The purpose of a dashboard is to view the most important KPI's and results at a glance. Dashboarrds are an interactive way to see how the company or project perform.

RESULT

Dashboards offer a streamlined way to uncover valuable insights effortlessly. On this dashboard, one can easily point out that sales improve towards Q4, which is a festive period. In all, dashboards empowers swift and informed decision-making.

PROJECT DURATION

On average, the development of a dashboard takes two weeks- a month. the development consists of two phases:

  • Selection of KPI's and design of the dashboard.
  • Collecting data and dashboard development.
