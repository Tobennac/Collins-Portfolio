    Time Series Forecasting

    Time series forecasting is the collection of data at regular intervals in terms of Days, Hours, Months, and Years. Time series forecasting is a technique in machine learning that analyzes data and the sequence of time to predict future events. This technique provides near-accurate assumptions about future trends based on historical time-series data.
